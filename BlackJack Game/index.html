How to Play Blackjack

  1. Start by placing your bet before the cards are dealt.
  2. Both you and the dealer will receive two cards. Your goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  3. Number cards are worth their value, face cards are worth 10, and an Ace can be worth 1 or 11.
  4. Choose whether to "Hit" (draw another card) or "Stand" (keep your current hand).
  5. The dealer must draw cards until they reach at least 17.
  6. If your total is closer to 21 than the dealer’s without exceeding 21, you win!

Blackjack is a game of strategy, luck, and knowing when to stop. Give it a try and see if you can beat the dealer!
